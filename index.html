Section A

The sun set behind the mountains, casting a golden hue over the valley. Birds chirped softly as they settled into their nests for the night. The air was cool and fresh, filled with the scent of pine and earth. It was a perfect evening for a quiet walk.

Section B

In the bustling city, people moved quickly through the streets, their faces illuminated by the glow of neon signs. The sounds of traffic and distant chatter created a symphony of urban life. Amidst the crowd, a street musician played a soulful tune, capturing the hearts of passersby.

Section C

The beach was serene, with gentle waves lapping at the shore. Seagulls soared above, their calls echoing in the salty air. Children built sandcastles while adults relaxed under colorful umbrellas. The ocean stretched out endlessly, a deep blue under the bright summer sky.

Section D

In the quiet library, the smell of old books filled the air. Sunlight streamed through the large windows, casting shadows on the wooden floors. Students sat at long tables, engrossed in their studies, while the librarian moved silently among the shelves, reshelving books with care.
